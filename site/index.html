Key Features

9 Country Presets

Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, Austria, United Kingdom, United States, France, Denmark, and Sweden. Each with full rating scale and conversion tables.

4 National Scrapers

Searches FSK, BBFC, Medieraadet, and Kijkwijzer websites directly when TMDB and OMDB come up empty.

Smart Conversion

Converts between rating systems using conservative rounding. Borderline cases always map to the stricter bracket.

Background Operation

Runs as a Kodi service with configurable scan intervals and rate limiting. No manual intervention needed after setup.

Retry Tracking

Items without a result are retried on every scan. After a configurable timeout, a fallback certification is applied.

Replace Mode

Optionally re-certify items that already have a rating, useful when switching countries or fixing mismatched scraper settings.

Get Started in 4 Steps

1
Install the addon

From the Rouzax Repository or download from GitHub Releases.

2
Enter your TMDB API key

Free to obtain at themoviedb.org.

3
Select your country

Pick from 9 presets. The addon loads the right rating scale and conversion tables.

4
Close settings

The addon starts scanning your library. Previously uncertified items get their ratings filled in automatically.
